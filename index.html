About

I am a Civil Engineer with over 5 years of experience in cost estimating, quantity take-off, project management, QA/QC, and site execution for commercial, residential, hospital, and infrastructure projects. My technical expertise includes using Bluebeam Revu and PlanSwift for quantity take-off, and Microsoft Excel for preparing detailed unit price analyses and BOQs. I am also proficient in Primavera P6, Project Libre, MS Project, AutoCAD, SketchUp, Lumion,and Enscape Working across both pre-construction planning and project execution has given me a deeper understanding of how estimates translate into actual project requirements. My background in project management and coordination has strengthened my ability to assess costs more realistically, anticipate potential challenges, and ensure that estimates are both accurate and practical. These combined experiences make me confident that I can deliver precise, reliable, and well-structured estimates that help clients save time, make informed decisions, and achieve successful project outcomes.
