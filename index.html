About

I am also proficient in Primavera P6, Project Libre, MS Project, AutoCAD, SketchUp, Lumion,and Enscape Working across both pre-construction planning and project execution has given me a deeper understanding of how estimates translate into actual project requirements. These combined experiences make me confident that I can deliver precise, reliable, and well-structured estimates that help clients save time, make informed decisions, and achieve successful project outcomes.
